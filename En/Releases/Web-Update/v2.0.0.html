Version 2.0.0

Released on: 06/20/24

Added

  • User Detailed Diagnostics Page

Detailed description

This update introduces the innovative User Diagnostics Page, providing users with comprehensive insights into their geolocation, device information, network system, and more. The page operates with zero data retention on page load, ensuring that no information is stored beyond the immediate session.

Access to the User Diagnostics Page is granted upon user consent, underscoring our commitment to transparency and empowering users with control over their personal information. Users can explore detailed insights into their system configuration, network environment, and geolocation data, gaining a deeper understanding of their digital footprint.

It's important to note that no data accessed through this page is stored, retained, sold, or downloaded. Information is fetched dynamically for immediate viewing, offering users a secure and educational experience. This feature enables informed decision-making about digital interactions, enhancing personal security and technical knowledge.

Additionally, our website is open-source, inviting public scrutiny and contribution. This openness reinforces our commitment to trust and accountability, allowing users to verify our practices and participate in improving our services.
