Resident Evil 2 Remake Exceeds 13.9 Million Units, Outselling Original

The Resident Evil 2 Remake has achieved a remarkable milestone by surpassing 13.9 million units in sales, outshining the original game's sales record. This makes it the best-selling title in the entire Resident Evil franchise.

Sales Milestone

The success of Resident Evil 2 Remake can be attributed to its faithful recreation of the classic survival horror game with modern graphics and gameplay mechanics. Fans of the series have praised its immersive experience and the nostalgic appeal of revisiting Raccoon City.

Community Reception

The gaming community has enthusiastically embraced Resident Evil 2 Remake, citing its intense atmosphere, gripping storyline, and enhanced gameplay elements. Critics have also lauded the game for its innovation while staying true to the original's core elements.

Future Updates

Capcom has announced plans to continue supporting Resident Evil 2 Remake with additional content updates and patches to enhance player experience. This includes new game modes, downloadable content (DLC), and improvements based on community feedback.
