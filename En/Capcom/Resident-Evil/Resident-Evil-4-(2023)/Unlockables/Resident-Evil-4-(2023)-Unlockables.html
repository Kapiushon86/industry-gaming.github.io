Resident Evil 4 (2023)

An in-depth look at the remake of a classic survival horror game.

Main Game Unlockables

Weapons

Weapon Weapons Specifications
Infinite RPG Complete the game, then is available at the Merchant for 2,000,000 Pesetas.
Chicago Sweeper Complete the game on Professional Mode with an A rank. Available for 1,000 CP in the Extra Content Shop.
Hand Cannon Complete new game on Professional mode without using any bonus weapons. Available for 1,000 CP in the Extras Content Shop. (Can also be done through Free Mercenaries DLC: Achieve S rank on each map with only 1 character)
Primal Knife Destroy all 16 Clockwork Castellans. Available for 1,000 CP in Extras Content Shop

Jacketless (Ashley) clear a specific chapter.
Armor (Ashley) Complete main story on Hardcore mode or higher with an A rank. (prevents damage from all sources or kidnapping on Ashley. If picked up she will be dropped immediately because of weight.)
